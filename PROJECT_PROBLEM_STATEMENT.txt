# GraduWay Project Brief

## Project Overview
GraduWay is a Flutter-based alumni-student guidance platform created for Aditya Engineering College. The app connects students, alumni, and administrators in one role-based system so students can get career guidance, alumni can share experience, and admins can manage the platform.

The project is designed as a digital bridge between college students and graduates. It includes separate experiences for students, alumni, and admins, with features such as alumni discovery, career roadmaps, Q&A, placement insights, event updates, and profile management.

## What Problem This Project Solves
In many colleges, students want practical career advice but do not have an easy way to connect with seniors or alumni who already faced the same academic and placement challenges.

The common problems are:
- Students do not know whom to ask for career guidance.
- Alumni are willing to help, but there is no structured platform to connect them with students.
- Career roadmaps, placement information, and mentorship are often scattered across different sources.
- College administration has no single place to observe engagement or manage community interaction.

GraduWay addresses these issues by bringing all three groups into one platform with clear role-based access and focused functionality.

## Problem Statement
Students often struggle to make informed academic and career decisions because reliable mentorship, placement awareness, and alumni connections are not organized in a single system. At the same time, alumni lack a simple and structured way to support current students, and administrators lack a unified platform to oversee these interactions.

Therefore, the problem is to design and build a centralized digital platform that:
- connects students with alumni for mentorship and advice,
- helps students explore career roadmaps and placement insights,
- allows alumni to answer questions and share guidance efficiently,
- and gives admins visibility into the overall platform activity.

## Why This Project Matters
GraduWay is useful because it reduces confusion for students, makes alumni support easier to access, and creates a more connected college community. Instead of relying on informal messages or disconnected social media groups, users can interact in a dedicated environment built for guidance and growth.

## Main Users
- Students who need career guidance, alumni support, and placement awareness.
- Alumni who want to mentor students and share professional experience.
- Admins who manage the platform and monitor user activity.

## Core Idea
The core idea behind GraduWay is to turn college experience into a connected support network. A student should be able to discover the right alumni, ask questions, follow a career roadmap, and stay informed about opportunities without leaving the app.

## Short Conclusion
GraduWay is not just a college app. It is a structured mentorship and career guidance platform that solves the gap between students who need support and alumni who can provide it.
